Our Service Areas

Oil and Gas Supply Chain Management Support

We provide comprehensive services across upstream, midstream, and downstream operations including engineering, scoping, Pre-FEED and FEED, compression, gas processing and storage, crude treating and transport, pipeline and SCADA, enhanced oil recovery, and petroleum product trading.

Engineering

Saterra delivers integrated EPC services including storage tanks and infrastructure, onshore pipeline construction, process and plant layout, installation and maintenance for civil, mechanical, structural, electrical, instrumentation, and automation systems.

Procurement

We supply valves, flanges, pumps, nozzles, seals, O-rings, bolts, nuts, OCTG, pipes, subsea systems, and provide HSE procurement, OEM representation, RFP management, evaluation, subcontractor negotiations, and purchase order administration.

Logistics

Our specialized logistics services include sea and air freight, barge operations, offshore delivery of cargo, fuel, water, and supplies, plus haulage, travel protocol support, and integrated project logistics.

Project Management and Technical Consultancy

We provide contract and scope management, personnel oversight, communication coordination, earned value monitoring, capital budgeting, risk mitigation, client engagement, and manpower support for cost-effective delivery.

Petroleum Storage, Terminaling and Trading

We provide industry-compliant oil and gas storage tanks and related infrastructure and leverage partnerships to trade middle distillates including LPG, naphtha, HFO, AGO, and other refined products.

Power Utilities and Renewable Energy

Saterra supports gas-fired and captive power, solar, wind, and battery projects, including infrastructure development, mini-grid design and commissioning, SCADA and gas control, substations, permitting, and startup support.

Saterra Energies will make proper provision for the health, safety and welfare of our people, clients/visitors and contractors, and those in the community who may be affected by our activities. Continual improvement is achieved by implementing our HS&E Management System and related standards.
